Berkeley Zoning Slider

This map shows the land use zoning of Berkeley, CA in 1949 (left) and 2018 (right). Slide the center button left and right and hover over the map to compare the changes in land use over the years. I made this map to accompany an article that I wrote about the racist/classist origins of Berkeley's zoning code and its effects on race and class-based boundaries and the housing crisis today.
