Density

This data visualizations displays the housing density and median household income (data from 2017 5-year American Community Survey) of the 50 largest "urban areas" (defined by the census) in the United States disaggregated by zip code. The coloring is scaled according all 50 urban areas such that the darkest color shade of red represents the 5% densest zip codes within all 50 urban areas. For a fullscreen version, click here.
