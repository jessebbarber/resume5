Bay Area Immigration Bond Fund Website

This is the website of the Bay Area Immigration Bond Fund – an organization that I helped start that works to free detained immigrants from ICE detention. I designed, created, and maintain this website. This has included the use of Squarespace, Sketch, Adobe Creative Applications, HTML/CSS/Javascript. In a year and a half of operation, we have fundraised almost $300,000 and helped free almost 50 people from detention. Link here.
